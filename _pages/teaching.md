I hold a B.A. in Economics from McGill University with six years of research
experience across language economics, housing policy, labour economics,
Indigenous economic measurement, and machine learning. I bring that range to
your coursework, high school through graduate-level, and to research
sound-boarding between sessions with your supervisor.

## Supported Subjects

- R / RStudio
- Statistics
- Microeconomics
- Macroeconomics
- Labour Economics
- Quantitative methods &amp; research design

I also act as a sound board for research in adjacent subjects between sessions
with your formal supervisor. This helps students make stronger impressions,
develop communication skills, build confidence, and get more out of their own
supervision by asking better questions.

## The Approach

Collaborative, adaptive, and holistic. I can help with anything from becoming
a more independent learner, to motivation, to extra hours of one-on-one time,
to debugging support. I tailor activities and lectures to the individual
learner using current literature in neuroscience and learning science, so your
time is used efficiently.

### 1. Free Consult

- Get a sense of fit
- Understand how you learn
- Get a sense of your needs
- Identify the accommodations and software needed to succeed 
- Learn the specific requirements to excel in your course
- Gauge your strengths and gaps as they pertain to the course

> I extend the duration of the consult free of cost to disabled students
> requiring additional time to coordinate their unique learning needs.

### 2. Follow-Ups (as needed)

Sessions run as long as you need them. Pacing, frequency, and depth get
calibrated to your course load and goals.

## Why This Exists

Education is my top priority and my commitment to professionalism is
unwavering.

your needs as a full person. Throughout our time together I will work to ensure you not only

Feel free to reach out with any concerns, questions, or suggestions,
